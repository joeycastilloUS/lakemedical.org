Primes are bare VMs with no software deployed.

The relay correctly decrypts, dispatches, and attempts to forward auth to prime. The prime VMs are RUNNING but empty — no binary, no service, no data directory. TCP connect times out after 5 seconds, relay returns "prime unreachable", Worker gets malformed response → 502.
