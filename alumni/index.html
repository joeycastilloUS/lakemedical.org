How It Works

1

Register Yourself

Sign up with passwordless TOTP authentication. Scan a QR code with your authenticator app and you're in — no passwords to remember or reset.

2

Create Your Institutional Account

Set up your college or university profile. Configure your branding, define your alumni engagement team, and establish your outreach workflow.

3

Import Alumni Data

Upload a CSV of your graduates from the registrar. The platform automatically enriches each record using the NPI Registry (practice location, specialty, license status) and PubMed (published research and citations) — turning a simple name list into a complete professional profile.

4

Engage & Track

Work through a prioritized outreach queue. Contact alumni, log interactions, and track engagement in real time. The system generates smart triggers — practice moves, new publications, milestones — so you always know who to reach out to and why.
